Vanilla Milkshake

Description

There is nothing better than a perfect milkshake. Luckily, making one at home is extremely easy. This simple milkshake recipe gives you the perfect ratio of milk to ice cream and is completely customizable!

Ingridients

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 c. milk
  • Whipped topping, for garnish
  • Sprinkles, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
